Our Story

Every Community Deserves The Opportunity To Thrive.

Founded in 2017, Vallabhi Foundation is a non-profit organisation committed to improving lives through education, healthcare, women empowerment, and community welfare initiatives across India.

Our work is driven by one belief:

"No one should be left behind because of their circumstances."
